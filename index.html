Course Goals


1. Consider the role of rhetoric, technology, and ethics in acts of making.

These projects all affected how I thought about our first goal. Thinking about accessibility really affected many of my design choices, specifically on Design Fuego. Creating a website with readable font, high contrast colors, and low load graphics is something I never focused on before. After our readings and class it really made me realize the ethics and responsibility we have as designers.

2. Leverage Design Principles.

I leveraged many design principles through these projects. The one that most stands out to me is the Good Design project. I had to analyze the principles of design and find them in everyday design work. It was there I can see what I like and what works fundamentally in design.

3. Engage with others in a workshop environment.

Workshopping because a regular routine through these projects. I found myself really listening to my peers critiques and suggestions with a careful ear. I edited a lot of my projects through the workshopping, but more importantly, it helped me find my style. Being able to hear what people like across all my projects has helped me narrow down my personal design style.



4. Practice revision within and across media forms.

Revising is key and within the UX Branding study, I practiced it a lot. Presenting a client with multiple options also helped me to learn what things I could take from the examples to create an even better design. Through revising I learned to trust other’s eyes and rely on the requirements instead of just my own opinion. These revisions made my project and my skills better.

5. Learn to present your work.

Presenting work is always scary, but practicing it with my Cartoon Project made it easier. I learned how to present my project and the step I took to arrive there. I walked the class through my process, initial ideas, and steps I took to achieve it. I described my illustrator skills to the class. I became more confident in my work because of this and will carry this skill for long after this class.
